§ 1

In which the usage of noobhack becomes clear

NetHack is hard. noobhack tries to make it a little easier. It remembers stuff about the Mazes of Menace that would otherwise be tedious for you to think about.

§ 2

In which noobhack reveals its usefulness

Found a rare bookshop but don't have the means to pay, the cunning to steal, or the muscle to murder? noobhack remembers features about the dungeon so you can come back later when you're a dragon riding looter.

Taught yourself a spell, but can't remember when it expires? noobhack's got you covered.
